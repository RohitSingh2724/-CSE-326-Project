One Piece

About the Author

Name:Eiichiro Oda

Eiichiro Oda is a Japanese manga artist best known for creating the immensely popular manga series One Piece. Born on January 1, 1975, in Kumamoto, Japan, Oda began drawing at a young age and published his first manga work at just 17. One Piece, which started serialization in 1997, follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy and his pirate crew as they search for the ultimate treasure known as the "One Piece." The series has gained worldwide acclaim for its imaginative storytelling, rich world-building, and diverse cast of characters, making Oda one of the most successful and influential manga artists in history..
